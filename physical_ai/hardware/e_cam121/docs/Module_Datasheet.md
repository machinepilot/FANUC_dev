# e-CAM121_CUMI412C_MOD Module Datasheet

**Revision 1.1** | e-con Systems | September 2025

---

## Overview

Low-power MIPI CSI-2 camera module based on Sony IMX412-AACK-D (STARVIS). 12.3MP, 1/2.3" format, 30 mm x 30 mm, S-mount lens holder.

---

## Features

- 1/2.3" optical form-factor, 12.3MP
- Resolutions: 4056x3040 @ 60 fps, FHD (2028x1112) @ 240/60 fps
- 30 mm x 30 mm, dual 20-pin SMT connector
- Power: 554.4 mW
- Operating temperature: -20°C to 60°C
- RoHS, REACH, FCC compliant

---

## Sensor (IMX412-AACK-D)

- 12.3MP RGB RAW 10/12-bit
- 1/2.3" optical form-factor
- Pixel: 1.55 µm x 1.55 µm
- Chip: 7.564 mm x 5.476 mm
- Electronic shutter

---

## Frame Rates

| Resolution | RAW 10 | RAW 12 |
|------------|--------|--------|
| 4056 x 3040 | 60 fps | 30 fps |
| FHD (2028 x 1112) | 240, 60 fps | — |

---

## Electrical

- 3.3V, 0.168 A typical
- Temperature: -20°C to 60°C (noise increases with temperature)
