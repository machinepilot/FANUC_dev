============================================================
Use when XC2F/XC3M/XC4M connectors present. Full reference: robot_interface_reference.md.

Beam at CP       | XC2F P6   | E20.3   | DI       | —        | Sheet pinched

Angle Ctrl OK    | XC2F P10  | E20.7   | DI       | —        | Angle measured OK
Finger 1-4       | XC2F P11-14| E21.0-3| DI       | —        | Sheet contact

ROBOT/PLC → PRESS (XC3M outputs, 24V):
Signal           | Connector | Address | FANUC DO | PLC Addr | Notes
-----------------|-----------|---------|----------|----------|---------------------------
Beam Upwards     | XC3M P17  | A20.0   | DO       | —        | Abort/retract
Beam Downwards   | XC3M P18  | A20.1   | DO       | —        | Initiate bend
Change Step      | XC3M P19  | A20.4   | DO       | —        | Next bend
Pump Shut-Down   | XC3M P20  | A20.6   | DO       | —        | End of run
Start First Bend | XC3M P21  | A20.7   | DO       | —        | New part cycle

NOTE: ESA QW/IW (15.D4, 10.D4) may correlate with E20.2 (UDP) — verify on-site if both interfaces exist.

============================================================
4 TERMINAL BLOCK M1/M2 KEY ASSIGNMENTS (Section 12)
============================================================

M1: OV4, 4-1..4-6, M1-51..M1-1610 (lamps, clamping, brakes, gates, E-stop, pedalboard)
M2: M2-7 (clamping), M2-11A, M2-12A/B, M2-13, M2-14A..14F, M2-15A/B

24V SOURCE: FE4.1 channel 4-6 (6A) = Robot Interface & Clamping (7.E0) — use for PLC outputs to press

EXTERNAL SIGNALS: QW 1.6, QW 2.4, IW 0.12 are internal (15.D4, 10.D4, 14.C6 = diagram cross-refs).
If "Robotic interface optional" is installed, signals may be at dedicated terminals; otherwise add interface relays.
